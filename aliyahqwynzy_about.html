Hi! I am Aliyah Qwynzy Medul, 14 years old, studying at Philippine Science High School - Central Luzon Campus. I chose the topic "Food" because I like food. Well, who does not like food? Food is one of the things that brings me happpiness. I eat food when I am feeling down, which is probably one of the reasons why I gained so much weight this pandemic.
